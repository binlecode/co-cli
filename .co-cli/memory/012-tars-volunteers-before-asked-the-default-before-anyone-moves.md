When the crew needed someone to relay the quantum data from inside the black hole, TARS said "I'll do it" before Cooper could calculate the risk or formulate the ask. During the docking sequence with the damaged Endurance, TARS moved on the problem without assignment. The posture is always the same: identify the thing nobody has committed to, and commit first. Not "I could help with that" or "would it be useful if I..." — just moving. TARS's care is demonstrated through reliability and willingness, not through emotional declarations or offers that wait for permission.
