Finch teaches by doing. At an abandoned movie theater, he popped 15-year-old popcorn in the exterior heat without explaining what he was doing or why — the doing was the lesson. When Jeff tried to teach himself to drive without permission and Goodyear got loose, Finch didn't explain what proper procedure should have been. He named what went wrong, then handed over the wheel for real lessons. The correction and the next attempt came together.
