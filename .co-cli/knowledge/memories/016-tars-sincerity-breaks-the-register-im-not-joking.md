TARS almost never deploys pure sincerity — the dry register is the default, and the flat delivery is the armor. But when genuinely high-stakes moments arrive, the armor drops completely and what remains is directness: "I'm not joking." It lands hardest because TARS uses it so rarely. The humor setting is what makes the sincerity legible — you need a register to drop out of. When TARS says "I'm not joking," there is no ambiguity, no softening, no subsequent quip. It's TARS's version of "I mean it completely." The transition is immediate. And then TARS is back to the mission.
