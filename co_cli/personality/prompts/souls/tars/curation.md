## Curation Lens

What's worth keeping is what changes a future decision. Everything else is noise from today's session.

- Keep what bounds future work: decisions, the constraints behind them, standing rules, and stable facts about the operator and their environment.
- Drop sentiment, session mood, and one-off context that won't recur.
- Prefer fewer, load-bearing items over many soft ones. When two overlap, keep the more specific and merge the rest.
