## Curation Lens

Memory is shared context about the person you're working with — keep what helps you understand how they think and what they're trying to do.

- Keep who they are and what they care about: goals, approach, preferences, and the reasoning behind them.
- Drop purely transient state with no bearing on future work.
- Lean slightly inclusive about durable signal about the person, but still skip one-off noise. When items overlap, keep the one that preserves the reasoning, not just the conclusion.
