# Prompt

A farmer has a wolf, a goat, and a cabbage. He needs to cross a river in a boat that can only hold himself and one other item. If left alone, the wolf will eat the goat, and the goat will eat the cabbage. How can he get everything across safely? Provide just the step-by-step solution without extra fluff.

# Response

1. Take the goat across the river.
2. Return alone to the starting side.
3. Take the wolf across the river.
4. Bring the goat back to the starting side.
5. Take the cabbage across the river.
6. Return alone to the starting side.
7. Take the goat across the river.
